How to get started.

  • Download the App: Find the PayPat app on your device's app store (available on iOS and Android) and download it for free. You can also access PayPat through our website on any desktop browser.
  • Create an Account: Follow the easy registration process to create your PayPat account. You'll need some basic information, and you'll be guided through our secure verification process.
  • Link Your Bank Account: Connect your existing bank accounts to PayPat for seamless transactions. We use secure connections to ensure your information is safe.
  • Start Transacting: Once your account is set up, you can start performing banking transactions right away. Whether you're transferring funds, paying bills, or setting up budgets, PayPat is designed to make it easy.
